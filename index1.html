Ton 618

The largest black hole ever found

And everything about it

What is Ton 618?

Ton 618 is the largest black hole found by humans. It was once thought to be a small blue star within the Milky Way, but it's actually one of the brightest known quasars.

How big is it?

Ton 618 is 66 billion M☉, with Schwarzschild radius (the radius of a black hole based on its mass) of 1,949 × 10¹⁴ meters, with a diameter of 389.8 billion kilometers, or 242.2 billion miles.

Where is it?

The cordinates of this black hole is RA 12h 28m 25s | Dec +31° 28′ 38″ , located in the Canes Venatici, 18.2 billion light-years away from Earth.

What are Black holes?

Black holes are enormous black spheres with gravity strong so strong, that it sucks in light and time . They are formed when a large star dies ( The fuel of the star runs out, and its own gravity squashes it into a black hole).
